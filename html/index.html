Lingap Recoletos: A Unified Force of Compassion and Hope for Typhoon Survivors

Uniting Recoletos Schools to Assist Typhoon Victims in Camarines Norte and Naga City

The Lingap Recoletos campaign, a collaborative initiative spearheaded by Fr. Rafael B. Pecson, OAR, and the four Recoletos schools in Luzon, provided much-needed aid to families affected by Typhoon Kristine in Daet, Camarines Norte, and Naga City. This first-ever unified donation drive, held from November 8 to 10, 2024, reached approximately 1,500 families, offering relief and hope in the aftermath of the disaster.
